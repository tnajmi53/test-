Evidence-Based Recommendations

Every track recommendation is grounded in peer-reviewed consumer behaviour research. Scoring draws on Milliman (1982, 1986) on tempo and retail behaviour; North, Hargreaves & McKendrick (1999) on cultural music congruence; Garlin & Owen (2006) meta-analysis of 32 music-retail studies; Knoferle et al. (2012) on multi-dimensional music-product congruence; Biswas, Lund & Szocs (2019) on atmospheric music in food marketing; and Lee & Jeong (2023) on audio-visual congruence across two million Instagram posts.
