Evidence-Based Recommendations

Every track recommendation in this tool is grounded in peer-reviewed consumer behaviour research. Scoring draws on Milliman (1982, 1986) on tempo and retail behaviour; North, Hargreaves & McKendrick (1999) on cultural music congruence; Garlin & Owen's (2006) meta-analysis of 32 music-retail studies; Knoferle, Spangenberg, Herrmann & Landwehr (2012) on multi-dimensional music-product congruence; Biswas, Lund & Szocs (2019) on atmospheric music in food marketing; and Lee & Jeong (2023) on audio-visual congruence across two million Instagram posts.

🔒 About Instagram Music Data

Instagram's full library (1M+ tracks) is behind authentication and cannot be bulk-extracted publicly. All tracks here have been individually verified to exist in Instagram's licensed audio library. Integration with the Meta Content Library API (requires academic approval) could enable live track counts in a future version.
